先进热管理实验室 - 研究助理

Research Assistant - Advanced Thermal Management Lab

2023年8月 - 至今 Aug 2023 - Present

导师: 张新荣教授, 北京大学

Advisor: Prof. Zhang Xinrong, Peking University

项目概述:

开创了一种新颖的三层混合框架（SPH微观建模 + SAC-DRL控制 + 安全监控器）用于三维堆叠芯片的动态热管理。

主要贡献:

  • 使用平滑粒子流体动力学（SPH）建模芯片热动力学，优化CUDA数据交互并降维以防止梯度爆炸
  • 为Soft Actor-Critic（SAC）算法设计多目标奖励函数，平衡温度、能耗和均匀性，动态优化Robin边界参数
  • 在ExHFT-11会议上展示研究成果，完整手稿正在准备提交至Applied Thermal Engineering（JCR Q1期刊）

成果:

  • 与传统PID控制器相比，峰值温度降低45%，冷却能耗降低25%，控制更新时间缩短至50ms（快75%）

Project Overview:

Pioneered a novel three-tier hybrid framework (SPH microscopic modeling + SAC-DRL control + safety monitor) for dynamic thermal management in 3D stacked chips.

Key Contributions:

  • Modeled chip thermal dynamics using Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics (SPH), optimizing CUDA data interaction and reducing dimensions to prevent gradient explosion
  • Designed a multi-objective reward function for the Soft Actor-Critic (SAC) algorithm to balance temperature, energy consumption, and uniformity, dynamically optimizing Robin boundary parameters
  • Presented the research at the ExHFT-11 Conference; the full manuscript is under preparation for submission to Applied Thermal Engineering (a leading journal in thermal engineering, JCR Q1)

Achievements:

  • Reduced peak temperature by 45%, decreased cooling energy consumption by 25%, and slashed control update time to 50ms (75% faster) compared to traditional PID controllers

复杂系统实验室 - 研究助理

Research Assistant - Complex Systems Lab

2022年9月 - 2023年10月 Sept 2022 - Oct 2023

导师: 李阿明教授, 北京大学

Advisor: Prof. Li Aming, Peking University

项目概述:

研究网络结构和博弈论机制在促进群体合作方面的共同演化。

主要贡献:

  • 复现和调试经典模型（如Fu Feng等），修复伙伴切换算法缺陷
  • 创新地将时间网络与声誉机制耦合，发现"弱记忆声誉更新（g = 1 - 5）"最大化合作频率
  • 构建了新颖的PD/PGG不确定选择模型，利用5D数据分析和3D热图识别最优合作参数（p = 0.3 - 0.5）

成果:

  • 证明时间网络比静态网络提高合作率15-20%，在复现PGG模型时达到>85%的相似度

Project Overview:

Investigated the co-evolution of network structure and game theory mechanisms on promoting group cooperation.

Key Contributions:

  • Reproduced and debugged classic models (e.g., Fu Feng et al.), fixing partner-switching algorithmic flaws
  • Innovated by coupling temporal networks with a reputation mechanism, discovering that "weak-memory reputation update (g = 1 - 5)" maximizes cooperation frequency
  • Constructed a novel PD/PGG uncertain choice model, utilizing 5D data analysis and 3D heatmaps to identify optimal cooperation parameters (p = 0.3 - 0.5)

Achievements:

  • Demonstrated that temporal networks boost cooperation by 15-20% over static networks and achieved > 85% similarity in replicating the PGG model
